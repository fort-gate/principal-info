## What is Fortgate?

**Fortgate** is a compliance-first platform designed to accelerate crypto adoption in Latin America — without sacrificing regulatory integrity.

We provide financial institutions, government agencies, and private enterprises with the infrastructure they need to transact in crypto confidently. Every transaction that flows through Fortgate is screened in real time against global blocklists and customizable internal compliance policies, ensuring your organization stays ahead of regulatory requirements.

Built natively on the **Stellar network**, Fortgate delivers fast, low-cost, and transparent compliance enforcement at scale.

Latin America is one of the fastest-growing regions for crypto adoption. But compliance remains the #1 barrier for institutional and governmental actors looking to enter the space. We give regulated entities the tools to:

## Core Features

### 🔴 Automatic Transaction Blocking
Every transaction is screened before it settles. Fortgate rejects any transfer originating from or destined to a flagged address — no manual review required, no delays, no second-guessing.

### 📋 Multi-Source Blocklist Enforcement
Fortgate screens against multiple authoritative sources out of the box:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **USDC Blocklist** | Centre's official list of sanctioned/frozen USDC addresses |
| **OFAC SDN List** | U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control Specially Designated Nationals |
| **Custom Internal Lists** | Your organization's own flagged addresses and entities |

### ⚙️ Customizable Compliance Policies
Through the Fortgate **Dashboard**, compliance teams can define, activate, and manage their own internal policies:
- Flag addresses by risk score, geography, or behavior
- Set transaction volume thresholds and velocity limits
- Create custom screening rules tailored to your institution's risk appetite
- Enable or disable policies without touching any code

### 📊 Compliance Dashboard
A centralized control panel where your compliance and operations teams can:
- Monitor transactions in real time
- Review blocked transactions with full audit trails
- Manage blocklists and policy configurations
- Export compliance reports for regulatory filings

### ⚡ Built on Stellar
Stellar's architecture makes Fortgate fast, affordable, and transparent:
- Near-instant transaction finality (~5 seconds)
- Ultra-low fees
- Native USDC support via Circle's Stellar integration
- Full on-chain auditability

## Security & Compliance Philosophy

At Fortgate, compliance is not a feature — it's the foundation.

- **Blocklist data is refreshed continuously** to reflect the latest OFAC and USDC updates
- **All policy decisions are logged immutably** for audit trail integrity
- **No transaction is settled** before passing all active screening layers
- **Access controls** ensure only authorized users can modify compliance policies

We operate on the principle that **crypto adoption at an institutional level is only sustainable if it is built on a compliance-first foundation.**
